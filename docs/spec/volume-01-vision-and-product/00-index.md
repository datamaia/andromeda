# Volume 1 — Vision, Problem, Scope, and Product

**Status:** Authored (draft) · **Owner:** Product (PRD area, per Volume 0 chapter 03)

Volume 1 defines what Andromeda is and why: the vision and the problems it answers, who it is
for, the concrete use cases, the product objectives (`PRD-NNN`, minted here), the nine product
principles in normative form, the authoritative phase definitions for the whole corpus, the
measurable success metrics, and the provided constraints, dependencies, and product risks
(`RISK-PRD-NNN`, minted here). Every requirement in Volumes 2–15 traces back to an objective in
this volume.

The official product tagline is: "Your terminal companion for shipping great software."
