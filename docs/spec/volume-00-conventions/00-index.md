# Volume 0 — Conventions and Document Control

**Status:** In authoring · **Owner:** Specification orchestrator

Volume 0 defines how the Andromeda Engineering Product Specification is written, identified,
validated, and changed. Its rules bind every other volume. No volume may redefine or contradict
a convention established here; conflicts are resolved by amending Volume 0 through the change
procedure in chapter 10.
