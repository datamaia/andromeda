# 07 — Register: Risks

Consolidated risk register, indexing every `RISK-*` identifier in the corpus. Risks are
defined (`### RISK-<AREA>-NNN — Name`) in their owning volumes using the template in
[chapter 02](02-normative-language.md); each definition carries the full metadata (Category,
Probability, Impact, Severity, Mitigation, Detection, Owner, Status). This register carries
the corpus-wide index: the linked ID resolves to the defining chapter, and the mitigation
summary is the Mitigation entry of the risk body. Every risk MUST have a mitigation; a risk
without mitigation is an audit failure. The corpus holds 114 risks; all are `Open` at
consolidation.
